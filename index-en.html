About ZherBulagy

ZherBulagy is an initiative to promote geothermal energy in Kyrgyzstan. We help households and businesses switch to eco-friendly heating, reducing CO₂ emissions and energy costs. Learn more about our courses and investment opportunities.

Benefits of Geothermal Energy

Eco-Friendliness

Geothermal energy reduces carbon emissions by 80% compared to traditional sources.

Cost Savings

Save up to 50% on heating and hot water costs annually.

Reliability

Geothermal systems operate consistently year-round.
