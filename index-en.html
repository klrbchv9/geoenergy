Geothermal Energy

Renewable energy source of the future for Kyrgyzstan

Our project promotes geothermal energy — a clean and inexhaustible energy source that uses Earth's heat to generate electricity and warmth.

Geothermal Energy is especially relevant for Kyrgyzstan, where there are significant reserves of this natural resource..
